FAQ

WHAT IS GROTTO?

Grotto lets you book an on-demand happy hour anywhere in NYC whenever you want for $20.

WHAT BARS ARE ON GROTTO?

GROTTO has partnered with some of the best bars in The City. For the most updated list, check your app - we're adding new venues every week.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

Choose a spot and purchase when you're ready to order your first drink. Show your timer screen to the bartender and place an order.

CAN I ORDER DRINKS FOR FRIENDS?

This is your personal happy hour. If friends want to join, just have them download the app!

WHAT CAN I ORDER?

What can I drink: Signature cocktails, beers, and most wines. No topshelf liquor or Dom Pérignon.

WHAT HAPPENS AFTER THE HOUR?

That’s up to you! Your tab is paid with tax and gratuity included.
